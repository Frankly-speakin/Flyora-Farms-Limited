About Us

Flyora Farms Limited is a modern agribusiness company specializing in commercial pig farming, poultry production, farm consulting, agricultural innovation, and sustainable farming solutions. Headquartered in Shendam, Plateau State, Nigeria, the company is committed to delivering quality livestock products, supporting food security, and promoting agricultural excellence.
