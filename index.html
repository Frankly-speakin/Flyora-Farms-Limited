About Flyora Farms

Flyora Farms Limited is a modern agricultural business based in Shendam LGA, Plateau State, Nigeria. We focus on poultry farming, exotic pig farming, crop production, and sustainable agriculture. Our mission is to produce high-quality farm products while promoting responsible animal care, food security, and modern agribusiness practices.
