About FlyoraFarms Limited

FlyoraFarms Limited is a modern agribusiness committed to sustainable livestock production, quality feed formulation, organic manure solutions and professional farm advisory services.

Located in Shendam LGA, Plateau State, Nigeria, we combine practical farming experience with innovation to help farmers, businesses and communities achieve sustainable agricultural growth.
