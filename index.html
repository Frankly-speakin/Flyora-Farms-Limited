About Us

Flyora Farms Limited is an agricultural business based in Shendam LGA, Plateau State, Nigeria. We focus on poultry farming, exotic pig farming, crop production, and sustainable agricultural practices. Our goal is to provide healthy farm products while promoting responsible farming, animal welfare, and modern agriculture.

What We Do

Poultry Farming

Healthy poultry raised with proper feeding, clean housing, and strong farm management.

Exotic Pig Farming

Quality pig breeds raised with good nutrition, hygiene, and animal care standards.

Fresh Produce

Fresh vegetables and agricultural products grown for homes, markets, and businesses.

Modern Agriculture

Using better planning, improved methods, and technology to support farm productivity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to produce healthy farm products through sustainable agriculture, responsible animal care, and modern farming practices. We aim to serve our community with quality poultry, exotic pigs, fresh produce, and trusted farm services.
