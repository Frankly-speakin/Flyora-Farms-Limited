About Flyora Farms

Modern livestock agribusiness rooted in quality, nutrition, and responsible animal care.

Flyora Farms Limited is a modern agricultural business based in Shendam LGA, Plateau State, Nigeria. We specialize in poultry farming, exotic pig farming, feed formulation, and sustainable livestock production. Our mission is to produce high-quality livestock products and nutritionally balanced animal feed while promoting responsible animal care, food security, innovation, and modern agribusiness practices. Through quality management, continuous improvement, and a commitment to excellence, we aim to contribute to agricultural development and support farmers with reliable livestock and feed solutions.
